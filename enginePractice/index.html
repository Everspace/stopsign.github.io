Actions and Resolve: Resolve is generated from the Motivate action, and "flows" downstream to the further actions. The primary resource of the action (resolve, mana, gold, etc.) is consumed at a specified rate per second on every action except Motivate. This consumption rate for progress cannot be stopped. You can only additionally add transferring the primary resource to a downstream action at a rate equal to your consumption rate, per downstream action. For example, if an action is consuming .1% resolve per second, it means that 50 times a second, it takes 1/50th of .1% of your resolve and adds the same amount to progress. If you are sending 100% to 2 downstream effects, it will be taking .1% (consumption) + .1% (stream1) + .1% (stream2) = .3% of current resolve per second (split into 50 parts). When progress fills up to the progress max, you get the on complete effect, which includes exp. When exp fills up, you get a level, with the described effects.
